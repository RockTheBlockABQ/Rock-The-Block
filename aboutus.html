About Us

DoloMyte Sounds is dedicated to assisting up-and-coming artists of all forms to showcase their art. If you are a graffiti artist, breakdancing artist, or a musical artist, come visit us on 4th and Central in Albuquerque, New Mexico. Our free communal event allows for artists to create a following and share their art with our community. DoloMyte Sounds consists of Michael Tafoya A.K.A Ekym1536 (host), Eric Tafoya A.K.A Dj E-Dub, and Hillery Tafoya (administrative assistant). During the summer months, we work in partnership with The MotherShip Alumni Art Gallery to provide these services to not only the artists but to our local community. This allows for the community to come together to support one another.

Check out our events, meet our featured artists, and don't forget to visit our gallery to see some amazing artwork! We look forward to seeing you all out there!
